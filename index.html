About Us

We are the nodal student body that overlooks all the institute's social activities.

We conduct HumaNITTy - the flagship event of social council, wherein the council engages in a week of chariable activites to celebrate the joy of giving. HumaNITTy is the Dhan Ustav's chapter in NITT.

Mission: To identify and address social issues in the campus and in the student community, through impactful initiatives, solutions and events.

Vision: To create a sense of social responsibility and awareness within the student community towards voluteering and self-initiating solutions to socital problems and working towards the betterment of the community.
